Thyme to Eat


About

Thyme to Eat is a vision created by Anh Pham and Hyerin Lee to provide users with an easy
and accessible way to create unique meals in an approachable amount of time. Along with this,
these founders wanted a platform that catered to all kinds of food lovers. Whether you are vegan,
vegetarian, or a full fledged carnivore, Thyme to Eat is perfect for you.
